It was a pretty frustrating day of coding for Kenny Wibowo.

Kenny was at a loss trying to code Connect Four.

You see, the tiles are supposed to gravitate all the way down...

... But instead they take their time, going one row per turn.

Looking for a solution, Kenny stared for so long that the lines started to blur.

Kenny was just about ready to punch his screen.

On the brink of defeat, Kenny turned to...

... His trusty robot, who was silent and not trusty at all.

"What did I expect," he thought, "he never changes."

"... No change? Wait, that's it! I forgot an important rule of programming..."

It's not a bug, it's a feature!

Kenny changed the name from Connect Four to Connect Fall...

And called it a day! The cycle continues...
